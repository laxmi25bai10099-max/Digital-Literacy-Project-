# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s ✅
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Think before posting content
- Verify information before sharing
- Maintain privacy settings
- Respect others and maintain a positive online presence

## Don’ts ❌
- Do not share personal information publicly
- Do not click on suspicious links
- Do not post offensive or harmful content
- Do not spread fake news
- Do not use weak passwords
