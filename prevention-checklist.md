# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Use strong, unique passwords for all your accounts.  
2. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.  
3. Do not click on links from unknown or suspicious emails/messages.  
4. Verify websites before entering personal information.  
5. Do not share sensitive personal information (Aadhaar, PAN, passwords) publicly.  
6. **Financial/UPI Safety:** Always check the recipient details before making UPI or online payments.  
7. **Financial/UPI Safety:** Avoid sharing OTPs, PINs, or banking passwords with anyone.  
8. Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security patches.  
9. Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi; use VPN if needed.  
10. Report any suspicious activity to the **National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)** or call **Helpline 1930**.
